About Me

I am an AI Researcher in ADD. I received my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from the KAIST Graduate School of AI, where I was advised by Prof. Kijung Shin. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from POSTECH. My research spans a broad range of AI applications, including data mining and computer vision for applied systems. Currently, I am working on object detection for aerial platforms.
